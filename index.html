What exactly is the problem?

Unfortunately this results in unfortunate accidents with pedestrians unknowingly stepping on them or vehicles running them over when they end up on the road. Leading to unnecessary deaths that could have otherwise been avoided if there was awareness of this problem. This only further serves to disrupt the local ecosystem of the pond, making this an issue worth writing about.
